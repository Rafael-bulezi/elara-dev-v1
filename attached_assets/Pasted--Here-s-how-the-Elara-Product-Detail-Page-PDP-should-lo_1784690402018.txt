**Here's how the Elara Product Detail Page (PDP) should look** — clean, trustworthy, mobile-first, and conversion-focused.

### Overall Philosophy
- Simple and trustworthy (avoid clutter)
- Strong focus on images and key information
- Easy to contact the seller (chat)
- Clear trust signals
- Good for Angola (Multicaixa mention, local delivery info)

---

### Desktop / Tablet Version Highlights

- Left side: Large image gallery (zoom + thumbnails)
- Right side: Sticky product info (title, price, variants, add to cart/chat)
- Below the fold: Full description, specifications, seller info, full reviews

---

### Key Sections & Best Practices

| Section                  | What to Show                                      | Why It Matters |
|--------------------------|---------------------------------------------------|--------------|
| **Image Gallery**        | Multiple high-quality photos + zoom              | Trust & decision making |
| **Price**                | Clear, big, with currency (Kz)                    | Immediate clarity |
| **Variants**             | Clean selectors (size, color, etc.)               | Reduce friction |
| **Delivery Info**        | Local (Luanda vs provinces) + estimated time      | Very important in Angola |
| **Seller Info**          | Name, verified badge, rating, link to profile     | Builds trust |
| **Chat Button**          | Prominent "Falar com o Vendedor"                  | Core of light model |
| **Description**          | Short first, then expandable                      | Mobile friendly |
| **Reviews**              | Preview + full section                            | Social proof |
| **Related Products**     | "Outros clientes também compraram"                | Increase order value |

---

### Extra Details That Make It Feel Premium

- **Trust signals** near price (Verified Seller, Secure Chat)
- **Stock status** ("Em stock" or "Últimas unidades")
- **Share button**
- **Add to Wishlist** heart icon
- **Smooth image zoom** on desktop
- **Fast loading** (optimize images)
